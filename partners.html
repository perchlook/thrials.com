Our Partners

Together, we can empower patients and revolutionize the future of healthcare.

We are highly collaborative and are open to partnering with a variety of groups. Our goal is to build a state-of-the-art product that helps biotechnology companies bring therapeutics as quickly as possible to patient communities.

By partnering with diverse partner groups, we can improve the efficiency and effectiveness of their matching process, ultimately leading to better outcomes for patients and researchers alike.

Partnership Opportunities

Patient Communities

These groups represent patients with specific medical conditions and can help match their members to relevant clinical trials. They also provide valuable insights into the patient experience and can advocate for patient-centered trial design.

Healthcare Providers

Partnering with healthcare providers allows us to put our innovative technologies to real-world use. By using our tool, healthcare providers can equip their patients with relevant trials and help facilitate their participation.

Research Institutions

Collaborating with research institutions provides access to cutting-edge research and clinical trials. These partnerships can lead to the development of innovative trial-matching algorithms and methodologies.

Community Organizations

Local community organizations can help raise awareness about clinical trials and reach underserved populations. They can also provide support and resources to participants throughout the trial process.

Government Agencies

Partnering with government agencies can help ensure compliance with regulations and guidelines. These agencies can also provide funding and support for clinical trials.

Industry Partners

Collaborating with pharmaceutical companies and other industry partners can help us access a wide range of clinical trials. Industry partners can also provide funding and resources to support the matching process.
