For Sponsors

Are you a sponsor or clinical investigator looking for the right patients for your clinical trials?

Key Advantages

As clinical science professionals, we understand the pain of patient screen failures and delays caused by the current workflows in patient recruitment. To help circumvent this significant pain point, we developed a solution to allow seamless matching of your trials to patients, thereby decreasing screen failure rates.

By empowering patients with the resources and knowledge, they become more motivated to complete follow-up visits, increasing retention rates. Our outreach program reaches out to diverse groups of patients, boosting diversity within your clinical trial.

Increase Patient Awareness

Many patients are unaware of clinical trials as a treatment option and the process of finding relevant trials. This lack of awareness can hinder patient enrollment and diversity in clinical trials.
